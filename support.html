Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

  • How do I start a breathing session? Open the app and tap "Start Session" to begin your breathing exercise.
  • Can I customize my breathing patterns? Yes! Premium users can customize breathing cycles, durations, and more.
  • How do I track my progress? Your sessions are automatically saved. View your progress in the app's statistics section.

Subscriptions & Premium Features

  • What's included in Premium? Premium includes unlimited sessions, custom breathing patterns, advanced statistics, and exclusive content.
  • How do I manage my subscription? Manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings (Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions).
  • Can I cancel my subscription? Yes, you can cancel anytime through your Apple ID subscription settings. Your premium access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Technical Issues

  • The app crashes or freezes. Try restarting the app or your device. If the issue persists, please contact us with details.
  • My progress isn't saving. Make sure you're logged in and have an internet connection. Check your account status in settings.
  • I can't restore my subscription. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase. Contact us if issues persist.

Account & Privacy

  • How do I delete my account? Contact us at realightua@gmail.com to request account deletion.
  • How is my data protected? We use industry-standard security measures. See our Privacy Policy for details.
  • Can I export my data? Yes, contact us to request a copy of your data.
